Why Choose Us

Snap is a brilliantly simple way to bring all your paperwork together in a standardised, digital format. Whether it’s a receipt, an invoice or a bank statement, upload it and Snap will extract the data you need, then send it to your accounting software, Xero. So you get the accurate numbers you need, without having to sit and enter them manually.

Our Clients upload invoices and receipts straight to Snap. And cloud storage means no physical storage costs or hefty spends on retrieving archived files.
